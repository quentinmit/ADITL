Groundrules

[1] Make sure the date and time on your camera are set correctly! We will use the timestamp embedded in the JPG file throughout the website. We have a test page where you can check that your camera is producing timestamps that we can read and see the date and time embedded in a given photo.

[2] Set your camera to take JPG photos (as opposed to TIF or RAW) of at least 1 megapixel. If you're a purist and must use TIF or RAW formats, please convert them to JPG before submitting photos (and PLEASE make sure that whatever you use to do the conversion preserves the EXIF tags--you can check this on our test page).

*We cannot accept digital images in a format other than JPG, those that have missing or incorrect timestamps, or those not taken in September 24, 2009.* So please double check everything.

[3] There's no specific upperbound on the resolution of images you submit (we'll just resize them), however, the total size of all the photos you submit must be TBD or less. We're not Google.

[4] We will have a web page where you can upload all your photos. More details on that later.

[5] Thanks for signing up, and don't worry, we'll remind you again just before Thursday.
